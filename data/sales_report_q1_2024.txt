SALES PERFORMANCE REPORT — Q1 2024
Company: DV Analytics Solutions Pvt Ltd
Report Period: January 2024 to March 2024
Prepared by: Sales Operations Team

==========================================================
EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
==========================================================

The company recorded total revenue of Rs 4.82 crore in Q1 2024, representing a 23% growth compared to Q1 2023 which was Rs 3.91 crore. The team closed 187 deals across all regions. The average deal size was Rs 2.57 lakh. The win rate improved from 34% in Q1 2023 to 41% in Q1 2024.

The South region led all regions with Rs 1.94 crore in revenue. The Enterprise segment contributed 58% of total revenue. The SaaS product line was the top performing product with Rs 2.1 crore in sales. Customer acquisition cost reduced by 12% compared to last quarter.

==========================================================
JANUARY 2024 — MONTHLY BREAKDOWN
==========================================================

January 2024 Total Revenue: Rs 1.42 crore
January 2024 Total Deals Closed: 54 deals
January 2024 New Customers Acquired: 21 customers
January 2024 Upsell Revenue: Rs 38 lakh
January 2024 Average Deal Size: Rs 2.63 lakh

January was a slow start to the year due to budget freeze at many enterprise clients. However the mid-market segment performed well with 31 deals closed. The SaaS product brought in Rs 72 lakh in January. The consulting services segment contributed Rs 41 lakh. Hardware and infrastructure sales were Rs 29 lakh in January.

Top performing sales rep in January was Arjun Nair from the South region who closed 9 deals worth Rs 24 lakh. The Bengaluru office led January with Rs 58 lakh. Mumbai office contributed Rs 47 lakh in January. Delhi office recorded Rs 37 lakh in January.

January had 3 deals lost to competitor Salesforce totalling Rs 18 lakh. Pipeline at end of January was Rs 12.4 crore across 312 qualified opportunities. January churn was low with only 2 customers not renewing, worth Rs 4.2 lakh annual recurring revenue.

==========================================================
FEBRUARY 2024 — MONTHLY BREAKDOWN
==========================================================

February 2024 Total Revenue: Rs 1.61 crore
February 2024 Total Deals Closed: 63 deals
February 2024 New Customers Acquired: 28 customers
February 2024 Upsell Revenue: Rs 52 lakh
February 2024 Average Deal Size: Rs 2.56 lakh

February showed strong recovery with a 13.4% month-on-month increase. The enterprise deals picked up significantly as budget cycles opened. The team closed 4 deals above Rs 10 lakh each in February. The largest single deal in February was with Tata Consultancy Services for Rs 18.4 lakh for an annual SaaS subscription.

The SaaS product brought Rs 89 lakh in February. Consulting services contributed Rs 47 lakh. Hardware sales were Rs 25 lakh in February.

Top performing sales rep in February was Priya Sharma from the West region who closed 11 deals worth Rs 31 lakh including the TCS deal. The Pune office had a breakout month with Rs 44 lakh, up from Rs 21 lakh in January. Chennai office contributed Rs 39 lakh in February. Hyderabad office recorded Rs 33 lakh.

The marketing team ran a webinar campaign in February that generated 84 new qualified leads. Email campaign conversion rate was 3.2% in February. The referral program brought 9 new customers in February worth Rs 14 lakh combined.

February pipeline grew to Rs 14.1 crore. February had 1 deal lost to a competitor worth Rs 9 lakh. Customer satisfaction score in February was 4.3 out of 5 based on 47 survey responses.

==========================================================
MARCH 2024 — MONTHLY BREAKDOWN
==========================================================

March 2024 Total Revenue: Rs 1.79 crore
March 2024 Total Deals Closed: 70 deals
March 2024 New Customers Acquired: 34 customers
March 2024 Upsell Revenue: Rs 61 lakh
March 2024 Average Deal Size: Rs 2.56 lakh

March was the strongest month of Q1 driven by end-of-quarter urgency and 3 large enterprise deals closing. Total revenue of Rs 1.79 crore was an all-time monthly record for the company. The team exceeded March quota of Rs 1.5 crore by 19.3%.

The three large enterprise deals in March were: Infosys deal worth Rs 22 lakh, HDFC Bank deal worth Rs 19 lakh, and Wipro deal worth Rs 16 lakh. All three were annual SaaS contracts with 3-year commitments.

The SaaS product brought Rs 98 lakh in March. Consulting services contributed Rs 53 lakh. Hardware sales were Rs 28 lakh in March.

Top performing sales rep in March was Vikram Singh from the North region with 13 deals worth Rs 38 lakh. Second was Sneha Reddy with 11 deals worth Rs 29 lakh. The entire North region team exceeded quota in March for the first time in 6 months.

The Bengaluru office had Rs 74 lakh in March. Mumbai office had Rs 56 lakh. Delhi office had Rs 49 lakh. Pune contributed Rs 35 lakh. Chennai and Hyderabad together contributed Rs 65 lakh in March.

March pipeline end-of-month was Rs 16.8 crore — a 19.1% increase from February. Deals lost in March were 4, totalling Rs 21 lakh. Average sales cycle length in March was 42 days, down from 51 days in January.
